Fresh Water

Fresh water marine animals are an amazing entry point into the fish keeping hobby and there are a variety to choose from. Some of the best beginner fish fall into this category such as platys, guppies, mollies, and tetras. But these fish are not simply just for beginners, grabbing a school of these fish brings a ton of life any fresh water aquarium! With enough experience, one can eventually start caring for other types of fresh water marine fish such as cichlids, angel fish, and corydoras!


Salt Water

Salt water marine animals are typically cared for by experienced fish keepers. Many steps need to be taken to correctly setup a saltwater tank, but we will help ease out the load of work! This category holds the most glorious of the marine animals, true deep sea beauties that will surprise all of your friends.Clown fish, the blood red fire shrimp, Harlequin Tusks, and the list just goes on and on.


Plants

Looking to add more life to your fresh water or salt water aquarium? Something that just gives your aquarium that natural look? How about a plant! Besides just looking great, there are a ton of benefits when adding plants to an aquarium: they reduce harmful bacteria and help with the production of beneficial bacteria, they are a natural source of oxygen, they provide cover for your fish, and can even be a food source!
